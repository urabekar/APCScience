Privacy

How we handle your information

APC Science is a small advisory firm. We keep this simple: we only use what you choose to send us, and we do not sell, share, or market against it.

What we collect

If you contact us — through the form, by email, or on LinkedIn — we receive the information you provide: your name, email, company, and the details of your inquiry. The contact form composes an email in your own mail application; nothing is submitted to a third-party server from this site.

How we use it

We use what you send solely to understand your request and reply to it. Inquiries are read and answered directly by the principal reviewing the engagement. We do not add you to marketing lists or automated sequences.

Analytics and cookies

This site does not set advertising or tracking cookies. Fonts are loaded from Google Fonts, which may receive standard request information (such as your IP address) when the page loads. If analytics are added in the future, this notice will be updated.

Sharing

We do not sell or rent your information. We share it only where necessary to respond to your request, or where required by law.

Your choices

You can ask us at any time to confirm what we hold, correct it, or delete it. Email amrit@apc.science and we will take care of it.

This notice may be updated as the firm grows. APC Science LLC · Boston, Massachusetts · amrit@apc.science
