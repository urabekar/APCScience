The brand at a glance

APC Science is a specialist consultancy for drug developers — shaping development strategy, planning, and operations across every modality and stage, and going hands-on with the chemistry and technical development of peptide, antibody, and conjugate therapeutics. The brand has to carry two things at once: the authority of a strategist and the precision of a chemist. Meridian is built for exactly that — calm, clinical, and credible, with a single teal signal that marks the point where science meets strategy.

The logo

The wordmark is the brand. “APC” is set in Inter at 800 weight; “Science” drops to 300 to create a confident contrast of authority and openness. Between them sits the teal dot — the fixed signal of the brand and the one element that always carries the accent color. Three approved lockups cover every context.

03

Construction & clear space

Protect the wordmark with clear space equal to the cap-height of “APC” (marked X) on all sides. Never place type, imagery, or edges inside this zone. Minimum sizes keep the dot legible: 120px wide for the primary logo on screen, 24px for the compact mark.

04

Logo misuse

The wordmark is fixed. These are the violations that erode it fastest. When in doubt, use an approved lockup at full integrity.

APCScience
✕ Don't stretch or distort

Never scale the wordmark non-proportionally.

APCScience
✕ Don't recolor the dot

The dot is always teal — never another hue.

APCScience
✕ Don't place on low contrast

Avoid busy or mid-tone backgrounds that swallow the mark.

APCScience
✕ Don't equalize the weights

“Science” must stay light (300) against “APC”.

APCScience
✕ Don't restyle the type

No italics, tracking changes, or substitute fonts.

APCScience
✕ Don't add effects

No shadows, glows, outlines, or bevels.

Typography

Inter is the single typeface across the brand — a humanist sans that is clean, authoritative, and highly legible at every size. (Suisse Int'l is the licensed equivalent if a distribution license is preferred.) One family, used with a clear hierarchy of weight and scale.

Set headlines tight (-0.02em) and never lighter than 700. Body stays at 400 with generous 1.65 line-height. Eyebrows and labels use wide tracking and uppercase to signal structure without shouting.

Translational pathway

08

Voice & tone

APC Science sounds like a senior operator who has actually built and run the thing — plain-spoken, precise, and candid. We earn trust with specificity, not adjectives. We never oversell, and we are comfortable saying what won't work.

Candid, not salesy

State the real situation and the real path. Confidence comes from precision, not hype.

We say

“Tell us what's in the way. We'll tell you, candidly, how we can help.”

Not

“Our revolutionary platform unlocks limitless potential.”

Operator, not advisor

Speak from having run platforms, GMP/GLP programs, and IND-enabling work — not from the sidelines.

We say

“Built on experience scaling research platforms and delivering thousands of projects.”

Not

“Industry-leading thought leadership and best practices.”

Precise & scientific

Use accurate terminology and concrete nouns. Name the modality, the stage, the constraint.

We say

“Peptides, conjugates, ligands, scaffolds, and non-standard chemistries.”

Not

“Cutting-edge molecules and next-gen solutions.”

Comfortable with the hard

Lean into difficulty. The unusual projects are the proof point, not a disclaimer.

We say

“The projects others find too hard are where we do our best work.”

Not

“We handle simple, standard requests quickly.”
